FAQ

Good to know before you visit.

Do you provide installation, or only supply materials?+
We handle both. Our in-house team fits everything we sell — panels, louvers, doors, wallpaper and grass — across Dhuri, Sangrur and nearby areas.
Can I see physical samples before ordering?+
Yes — the showroom near Dr. Balian Hospital carries physical swatches of every range so you can check texture, tone and finish in person.
Do PVC and WPC panels work in humid or bathroom areas?+
Yes, that's exactly what they're built for. Our WPC doors and PVC panels are moisture- and termite-resistant, making them ideal for bathrooms and kitchens.
Do you offer custom digital printing on panels or wallpaper?+
Yes — bring your design, logo or preferred pattern and we'll print it to size on panels or wallpaper.
How do I get a quote for my room?+
Message us on WhatsApp with your room size and product interest, or visit the showroom for an on-the-spot estimate.
Do you deliver outside Dhuri?+
We regularly deliver and install across Sangrur district and nearby parts of Punjab. Call ahead to confirm your area.
